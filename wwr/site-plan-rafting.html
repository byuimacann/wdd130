Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to provide my viewers with tried and tested destinations, reviews, safety and other pertinent information to help them select their next white water rafting adventure based on their preference and level of experience. I will have equipment rentals and rafting tours available to accommodate all rafting needs. A professional website will help me create an online presence and help build and expand my brand. Its ease of use, quality information, and appealing design will keep my customers—and their family and friends—coming back to use us again and again.

Audience

I feel white water rafting appeals more to a younger demographic. My target audience will be men and women ranging in age from 18-45 years old that enjoy outdoor activites and adventure.
