Overview

Purpose

Providing delicious cakes and pies to appease those daytime and late night cravings...both big and small. Our website makes ordering a snap. We are open late and have covenient drive-thru service for pick-up.

Audience

Our desserts appeal to all ages young and old but my target audience is females between the ages of 15-50 who like cake and/or pie or knows someone who does.

Home page

Delicious cakes and pies at the ready! No need to wait for hours while your cake or pie bakes and cools for you to indulge that sweet-tooth craving. Choose from one (or more) of our many delicious flavor options...we are sure to have something that will hit that sweet spot. We are open early and close late so that you can have your cake AND eat it too!

Cakes

Whether you are looking for something sweet to celebrate a special occasion or a special someone, we are here for you. Our cakes and cupcakes are made fresh from our bakery each morning. We have several different flavor options to appease the pickiest of pallets. Choose from our tried-and-true menu items or mix-and-match to create your own cakey concoction. Whether you need a cake to feed a crowd or just want a single cupcake to enjoy, we have got you covered.

Our everyday flavors options include:

  • vanilla
  • chocolate
  • red velvet
  • orange
  • strawberry
  • cotton candy
  • lemon
  • raspberry
  • almond
  • blueberry
  • lime
  • banana
  • coconut
  • cookie & cream
  • carrot
  • s'mores
  • guava
  • watermelon
  • s'mores

Seasonal favorite are available for limited times throughout the year. Make sure you check back often so you don't miss out on any of the yumminess!

Pies

Oh my...so much pie! We pride ourselves on our scrumptuous pies made from scratch each morning and baked with fresh, top-quality ingredients with you in mind. Come delight in a warm slice of pie a la mode or purchase a whole pie to share with family and friends. Make yourself and your mouth happy...you just can't beat down-home goodness and flavor...Yum!

Our everyday flavors include:

  • peach
  • pear
  • dutch apple
  • cherry
  • blueberry
  • 3 berry
  • raspberry rhubarb
  • banana cream
  • french silk
  • coconut cream
  • key lime
  • peanut butter
  • strawberry
  • lemon cream or meringue

Make sure you check back during the holiday season to order your seasonal favorites including pumpkin and pecan. We 'pied' ourselves on making the holiday season that much warmer for you and yours.
